About

Have you ever gone to the dispensary and felt extremely overwhelmed with all of the options?

Or maybe you felt rushed through the process and decided to pick a random strain you knew nothing about?

This application is here to fill those gaps.

It will inform you on the various effects of a given strain, educating you on what to expect. This is also a great application for getting ideas of potential strains to purchase next.
